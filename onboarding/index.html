Pro tips

Get the most out of every conversation

Be specific. "Draft a reply to Maria's email about the shipping delay, keep it firm but professional" beats "help me with email."
Give context once. Your agents remember. You don't need to re-explain your business every session.
Reply to morning briefs. Even a quick "looks right" or "add X to the list" keeps the system accurate.
Let the Friday review guide you. The 3 improvement suggestions compound over time. Try at least one each week.
Correct mistakes. If an agent gets something wrong, say so. It writes the correction to memory and won't repeat it.
Read the evening sweep. Two minutes before bed sets up tomorrow. It's the highest-ROI habit in the system.
