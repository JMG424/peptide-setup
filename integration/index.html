Overview

What we're connecting

Three integrations. All read-only. Your agents can see your email, calendar, and iMessages — but they can never send, delete, or modify anything without your explicit approval.

Gmail — Read Only
Google Calendar — Read Only
iMessage — Read Only
🔒

Security-first approach

Before connecting anything, we harden your system's security — rotate credentials, restrict permissions, and lock down access. Your agents can draft replies and suggest changes, but every action requires your explicit approval. Nothing happens without you.

What this does NOT do

Your AI team CANNOT send emails, delete emails, create calendar events, accept invitations, or modify anything in your Google account. Read-only means read-only. We can add write permissions later if you want them — but only with your explicit approval.

Step 3

iMessage integration

BlueBubbles connects your iMessage to OpenClaw — running locally on your Mac, data never leaves the machine. Your AI team gains messaging context while respecting personal boundaries.

3a
Install BlueBubbles
You + Jason
Download and install BlueBubbles on your Mac. Sign in with your Apple ID. This creates a local bridge between iMessage and your AI team — everything stays on your machine.
⏱ 10 minutes

What you do

1. Download BlueBubbles from bluebubbles.app
2. Install and open the app
3. Sign in with your Apple ID (the one you use for iMessage)
4. In Settings: enable "Web API" and set a server password
5. Set up Google Firebase (BlueBubbles walks you through this)
6. Note the server URL shown (e.g., http://127.0.0.1:1234)

🔒

Privacy note

BlueBubbles runs 100% locally on your Mac. Your iMessage data never leaves the machine. The AI team can read messages for context but cannot send iMessages on your behalf. Personal messages are never referenced in business briefs unless you specifically ask.
